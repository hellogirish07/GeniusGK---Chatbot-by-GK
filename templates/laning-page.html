About GeniusGK

GeniusGK is your friendly, AI-powered chatbot built on the advanced Gemini API by Google. Whether you're a developer, student, or just curious, GeniusGK is here to help you code, learn, brainstorm, or simply have a fun chat!

  • Natural Conversations: Enjoy smooth, human-like interactions that adapt to your style and needs.
  • Smart Coding Help: Get instant code explanations, formatting, and even copy code blocks with a single click!
  • Modern & Interactive UI: Experience a sleek, responsive design with light/dark modes and playful idle messages to keep things lively.
  • Privacy First: Your chats are never stored. Everything stays between you and GeniusGK.
  • Powered by Flask & Gemini: Fast, secure, and always up-to-date with the latest AI tech.
